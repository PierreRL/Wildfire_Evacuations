\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

\textbf{Limitations:} Among the main limitations is the data. The join between fires and evacuations is imperfect, and the removal of unmatched evacuations may bias results. Further, the data is observational and thus subject to confounding and bias. Ideas around `remoteness' are hard to capture and may be a confounder. The model is also limited in its ability to capture the complex decision-making process around evacuation, which may involve many unobserved factors. Further, not all fires are recorded in the data, and not all evacuations are recorded, which may bias results. A `hurdle' model that accounts for the fact that some fires are not reported may be a useful extension.
